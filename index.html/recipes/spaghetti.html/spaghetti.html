Spaghetti

Classic Spaghetti

To make spaghetti you will need:

Ingredients

    Spaghetti noodles
    Pasta sauce
    Meat of your choosing

That's it!

Optional:

    You can make your own pasta sauce!
    You can find a recipe for homemade meatballs here!
    Reserve a little pasta water to add to your sauce!

Steps:

  1. Boil water, add salt and a little olive oil
  2. Add in your spaghetti noodles, cook until al dente
  3. Top with your sauce and meat of choice!

This always pairs well with a fresh salad or garlic bread!
